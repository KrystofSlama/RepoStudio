Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 30, 2026

Overview

RepoStudio is a macOS application for working with local Git repositories and Markdown files.

No data collection

RepoStudio does not collect personal data, usage analytics, diagnostics, advertising identifiers, or account information from the app.

No data sharing

RepoStudio does not sell, rent, or share data with third parties.

Local files

The app works with files and repositories that already exist on your Mac. Those files stay under your control on your device.

External links

If you open external websites from RepoStudio (for example GitHub), those websites operate under their own privacy policies.

Contact

For privacy questions, open an issue at github.com/KrystofSlama/RepoStudio/issues.

Policy updates

This policy may be updated from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by updating the effective date on this page.
